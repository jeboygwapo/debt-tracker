Features

Everything you need to get debt-free

🎯

Avalanche Payoff Engine

Pay minimums on everything, throw every extra peso at your highest-interest card first. Mathematically optimal — saves the most money.

📈

Balance Trend & Projections

Visual charts show your actual debt declining month over month, plus a projected payoff curve so you can see the finish line.

💱

OFW Mode

Earn in SAR, AED, USD, or any foreign currency? Set your exchange rate and the app converts your remittance to PHP automatically.

🤖

AI Debt Analysis

Powered by GPT-4o-mini. Get plain-language insights on your debt situation — what's costing the most, and what to do about it.

📋

Monthly Budget Planner

Enter how much you're sending home this month and instantly see how to split it across all your cards and loans.

🔒

Private & Self-Hosted

Your financial data stays yours. No third-party tracking, no selling your data. Deploy on your own server or use Fly.io.

How It Works

Up and running in minutes

1

Add your debts

Enter each credit card and loan — name, APR, and whether it's a fixed installment. One-time setup.

2

Log balances each month

Takes under 2 minutes. Statement balance, minimum due, due date, and what you actually paid.

3

Follow the plan

The dashboard tells you exactly which card to attack this month and how much to put on each one.

4

Watch the debt drop

Charts track real progress. The payoff planner projects your debt-free date so you always know how far you've come.
